About Me

Hi, thanks for checking out my site! My name is Alex Maranto, and I'm an aspiring software engineer.
I've worked primarily in the hospitality and entertainment industries, but during the COVID lockdown
I was able to get a remote job doing music research for Meta's Music Research team.
After getting a taste of the WFH life, I decided to enroll in the full-stack software engineering
course at Coding Temple once my project ended, in order to expand my career opportunities!

My primary focus has always been music. I started playing the violin when I was 9, and have since
learned how to play as many instruements as possible. I've worked as a music director at a church,
and have had the good fortune to be able to go on multiple full U.S. tours, with a few different bands.
